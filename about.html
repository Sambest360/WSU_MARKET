About WSU Small Business Services

Welcome to WSU Small Business Services, a dynamic platform designed to foster entrepreneurship and support the diverse talents of Walter Sisulu University students. Our initiative aims to create a thriving ecosystem where student-led businesses can flourish, providing valuable services to the university community and beyond.

At WSU Small Business Services, we believe in the power of student innovation and the importance of practical, real-world experience. By connecting skilled student entrepreneurs with customers in need of various services, we create a win-win situation that benefits both parties and contributes to the overall growth of our university community.

Our Mission

To empower WSU students by providing a platform that nurtures entrepreneurial spirit, facilitates skill development, and creates opportunities for practical business experience within the university ecosystem.

Our Vision

To be the cornerstone of student entrepreneurship at Walter Sisulu University, fostering a new generation of innovative, socially responsible, and successful business leaders.

From web development and graphic design to tutoring and food services, our platform showcases a wide array of student-led businesses. We are committed to maintaining high standards of quality and professionalism, ensuring that every service offered through our platform meets the needs and expectations of our customers.

Join us in our mission to transform student ideas into successful ventures, and experience the unique blend of creativity, enthusiasm, and expertise that WSU Small Business Services has to offer.
